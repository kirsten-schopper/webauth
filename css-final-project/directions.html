Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin, is located in the heart of the Midwest, approximately 20 miles northwest of Milwaukee. Situated amidst the scenic beauty of Wisconsin's countryside, the village is easily accessible via major highways such as Interstate 41 and Highway 45. Surrounded by lush farmlands and wooded areas, Menomonee Falls embodies the serene ambiance of rural life while still offering convenient proximity to urban amenities. Its central location within the greater Milwaukee metropolitan area makes it a desirable destination for both residents and visitors alike.
